Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

At RareShop, we strictly inspect every laptop, phone, and device before dispatching it to you. However, we understand that issues can happen. Our 30-Day Return Policy ensures you are fully protected if you receive a defective or damaged product.

1. Eligibility for Returns

You may request a return or replacement within 30 days of delivery if:

  • The product arrived damaged or scratched.
  • The product is defective (hardware/software fault) upon arrival.
  • You received the wrong item or model.
Note: We do not accept returns for "Change of Mind" or if the device has been damaged by the user (drops, water damage, or electrical surges).

2. How to Request a Return

  1. Record Proof: Please record an unboxing video or take clear photos of the defect immediately upon delivery.
  2. Contact Us: Send the proof to our support team via WhatsApp (+92 343 0821414) or Email (businessrareshop1@gmail.com).
  3. Verification: Our team will review your case within 24-48 hours.

3. Refund Timeline

Once your return is approved and the item is received back at our warehouse:

  • We will inspect the device to confirm the fault.
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
  • The amount will be transferred to your Original Payment Method (Bank Transfer, Easypaisa, or NayaPay).

4. Return Shipping

If the fault is ours (damaged/wrong item), RareShop covers the return shipping cost. We will arrange a pickup or reimburse your courier charges.
