RareShop Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

RareShop is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website [https://rareservices.store/] and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to process your orders and manage your account:

  • Personal Identification Data: Name, shipping address, email address, and phone number (required for shipping and order confirmation).
  • Payment Data: We facilitate payment through third-party platforms (Easypaisa, NayaPay, Digitt+). We **do not** store your sensitive financial or card details. We only process confirmation of funds received via screenshot verification.
  • Account Data (via Firebase): Your email address and encrypted password or Google profile information (Name, Profile Picture) if you sign in using Google. Your unique User ID is stored to manage your cart and order history.
  • Order Data: Details of products purchased, quantities, total price, and order date.
  • Usage Data (via Google Analytics): Non-personal data such as browser type, pages viewed, and site visit duration (used solely for improving our service and analytics, as seen in our admin dashboard).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders and manage shipments across Pakistan.
  • To verify payments (via WhatsApp screenshot verification).
  • To manage your account and personalize your shopping experience.
  • To communicate with you regarding your order status, shipping updates, and customer support.
  • To monitor site traffic and measure sales performance (via Google Analytics).

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We only share your information as necessary to complete your order:

  • Courier Partners: Your name, address, and phone number are shared with courier services (TCS, Leopards, M&P) for delivery purposes.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required to do so by law.
  • Firebase: Your account data (email, UID) is managed and secured by Google's Firebase platform.

4. Data Security

We employ technical and organizational security measures, including using Google Firebase and Firestore, to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure. All sensitive communication is encrypted (HTTPS).
